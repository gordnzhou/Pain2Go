Lower Back Mobility & Strength Exercises

Exercise 1: Cat-Camel Stretch

The cat-camel stretch helps improve flexibility and mobility in the lower back. Start on all fours with your hands directly under your shoulders and your knees under your hips. Arch your back up towards the ceiling like a cat, then lower your back down and lift your chest towards the ceiling like a camel. Repeat this movement for 10-15 repetitions.

Exercise 2: Bird Dog

The bird dog exercise targets the muscles in your lower back and core. Begin on all fours with your hands directly under your shoulders and your knees under your hips. Extend your right arm forward while simultaneously extending your left leg backward. Hold this position for a few seconds, then switch sides. Repeat for 10-12 repetitions on each side.

Exercise 3: Superman

The superman exercise strengthens the muscles in your lower back and glutes. Lie face down on a mat with your arms extended in front of you. Lift your arms, chest, and legs off the ground simultaneously, keeping your neck in a neutral position. Hold this position for a few seconds, then lower back down. Repeat for 10-12 repetitions.
