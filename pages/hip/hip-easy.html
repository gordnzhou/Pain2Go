Bridges (Beginner)

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Begin in a standing position with your feet hip-width apart.
  2. Take a step forward with your right foot, making sure to maintain a comfortable and natural stride length.
  3. Lower your body by bending both knee.

Single Leg Bridges (Advanced)

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Lie on your back on a mat with your knees bent and feet flat on the ground.
  2. Extend one leg straight out in front of you, keeping it in line with your hip.
  3. Keep your arms at your sides, with your palms facing down.
  4. Engage your core by pulling your navel towards your spine.
  5. Lift your hips towards the ceiling, forming a straight line from your shoulders to your knee.
